Report: act_report¶

WeRateDogs is an exciting twitter page that humorously rates dogs of different breeds and stages. A feature unique to these ratings is how dogs are rated above the rate-scale which is 10. These ratings can be as high as 15, even more. This, according to the handler is because they are good dogs.

An archive of WeRateDogs' tweets which was readily available, a dataset which contains dog_breed prdictions by a neural network of the rated dogs and a last dataset that contains favorite and retweet count queried via Twitter API were wrangled to obtain a single and high-quality dataset which included original ratings only. An exploratory analysis was carried out on the final dataset to get some insights too. This led to asking the following questions;

  1. What year did the handler make the most ratings?

Year 2016 had the highest number of ratingss of the three years. About 1000 dogs were rated in this year.

  1. What percentage of the dogs had a rating of more than 10?

57.93% of the dogs rated had a rating of >10.

  1. Which are the top 5 most rated dogs breeds?

The top 5 most rated breeds of dog are;

  1. Golden_Retriever 155
  2. Labrador_Retriever 103
  3. Pembroke 94
  4. Chihuahua 90
  5. Pug 62

  1. Which dog stage was mostly rated?

    According to the Dogtionary,

    Doggo: an older version of a pupper, a dog that understands tasks.

    Pupper: a younger doggo, inexperienced, unfamiliar or unprepared for responsibilities associated with being a doggo.

    Puppo: a transitional phase between a pupper and a doggo.

    Floofer: a dog that has excess fur.

Most of the dogs rated were in the Pupper dog stage.
